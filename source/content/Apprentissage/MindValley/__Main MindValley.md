## Understanding your WHY
When you are clear on what you want, you can live your life on purpose and with purpose.

Deep change comes through repeated practice. To create motivation in your practice, begin with the end in mind. It starts with intention.
1. Why did you start?
2. I AM ... *intention setting.*
3. 6 Levels of Why technique
	1. Start with your I AM statement and answer why it is important to you. 
	2. Then, take why it is important to you, and continue with that answer in the next statement.
	3. Ex:
		1. I AM ... is important to me, because ... (b)
		2. (b) is important to me, because ... (c)
			1. repeat it 5 time
